In-Store Recycling

Drop off your used working or non-working electronic devices or cables and we'll recycle them for free!

In 2009, the US discarded 2.37 million tons of E-Waste, whit only 25% recycled. By 2019, this rose to 6.9 million tons but just 15% got recycled. Electronics often contain harmful toxins valuable non-renewable resources that end up wasted. That's why recycling is crucial, and we're here to help!

Mail-In Recycling

Mail in your used working or non-working electronic devices or cables and we'll recycle them for free! Note that the customer is responsible for shipping costs and safely packaging batteries or other hazardous materials.
