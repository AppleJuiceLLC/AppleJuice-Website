Our Environment.

Let's work together to leave the world better than we found it. After all, teamwork makes the dream work.

In-Store Recycling

Drop off your used working or non-working electronic devices or cables and we'll recycle them for free! Note that the customer is responsible for safely packaging items or other hazardous materials.

In 2009, the US discarded 2.37 million tons of E-Waste, with only 25% recycled. By 2019, this rose to 6.9 million tons but just 15% got recycled. Electronics often contain harmful toxins valuable non-renewable resources that end up wasted. That's why recycling is crucial, and we're here to help!

Mail-In Recycling

Mail in your used working or non-working electronic devices or cables and we'll recycle them for free! Note that the customer is responsible for shipping costs and safely packaging batteries or other hazardous materials.
